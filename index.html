About Us


We are family owned business, manufacturing garment for some of the most respectable world fashion brands, since 2001. Our team consist of highly skilled craftsmen, some of them with more then 30 years of experience in various apparel production phases. We are dedicated to quality, precision and flexibility. These qualities qualified us for production of clothing for some of the world's most demanding fashion markets (France, German, Italy).

Depending on your project requirements, we are able to provide cut/sew as well as full package services. We welcome established brands, as well as a new fashion startups.
